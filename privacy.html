Privacy & Heartfelt Terms

We're Just Happy You're Here

We built ColorCheck with a simple goal: to make your design workflow smoother and a little more colorful. We are absolutely thrilled that you've chosen to use it! Whether you're fixing a personal project, studying design, or building the next big thing, we hope this tool simplifies your life.

For Our Corporate Friends

Yes, companies and enterprises are more than welcome to use ColorCheck! If our extension helps your business shine, we kindly ask for a friendly mention where appropriate. It's not a strict rule, just a request—spreading the word helps us keep the lights on and the colors bright for everyone.

Support the Craft

ColorCheck is free to use, and we intend to keep it that way. However, if you find it indispensable and want to show some love, we’d be over the moon if you considered a donation ❤. It keeps the coffee brewing and the updates coming.

Your Data Stays Yours

Technically speaking: We do not collect, store, track, or sell your personal data. Your hex codes, history, and palettes live on your device and nowhere else. It's your creativity, we just provide the eyedropper.
